Wibber





Check Spotprices on Electricity and your own Power usage. Wibber is a standalone App for Apple Watch and gets its data from Tibber. You have to be a Tibber customer to use this App.

Installation Manual


1. Signup for Tibber. link
2. Create an Access Token for your account. link
3. Install Wibber. link
4. A nickname for your home needs to be created in Tibber. This is created in the Tibber iPhone app: My Account -> My Home -> Nickname.
5. Enter Tibber Access Token.

The easisest way to enter the Token, is to create a Text Replacement for it on the Iphone. This is done in Settings->General->Keyboard->Text Replacement. When the prompt appears in the Watch then enter the text replacement phrase in the iPhone instead. On my iPhone I have created atjhez as the replacement phrase:



6. If your Tokens is valid you can now press it and enter into the App.
7. The App will not show info in several Tabs. First tab looks like:

This tab shows the Spotprice (including price addition, tax and VAT). Textcolor is Green if the price is considered "VERY_CHEAP" or "CHEAP", Blue if "NORMAL" and Red if the Spotprice is "EXPENSIVE" or "VERY_EXPENSIVE".


8. The sedond Tab looks like:
       
This chart shows the Spotprice on the Y axis and time on X. Tap on the chart to toggle time.


9. On of the Tabs just contains info about your Tibber acocunt and the App version,
10. If you subscribe to Wibber+ then you will also get Complications, "Plugin Planning", Power usage bar charts and charts for your costs by hour/week/month/year.
Complications

First picture shows the Corner Complication.
Second shows the Rectangular Complication.
Third the inline inline widget.
Fourth the Circular Complication with text. The text is your cost for power for this month excluding today.

Plugin planning

Use the crown to step number of hours, then this Tab will show you the optimal time to plugin in order to minimize the average spotprice..




ELectricity Cost

Your cost for Electricity for today, this week, month and a year back.




Power Consumption

Your Power usage for Electricity for today, this week, month and a year back.
