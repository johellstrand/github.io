WIBBER

Show the price for Electrical Power, your usage stats, your solar panel production, costs and much more. Wibber is developed for Apple Watch and gets the data from Tibber. You have to be a Tibber customer and get the corresponding Access Token in order to use the App. 

Video showroom

Price Charts

Todays and tomorrows, last weeks, last month and last years price for electricity is shown. The digital crown changes view. Drag gesture reveals the price for a specific point in time. The shadowed area below the graph stops at the current time.

Clock View

Shows current price in the center. The coming 11 hours price is shown as analogue clock. The color is green if the price level is "CHEAP" or "VERY_CHEAP", blue if "NORMAL" and red if the price level is "EXPENSIVE" or "VERY_EXPENSIVE". The price level is based on trailing price average (3 days for hourly values and 30 days for daily values).

Value Description
NORMAL The price is greater than 90 % and smaller than 115 % compared to average price.
CHEAP The price is greater than 60 % and smaller or equal to 90 % compared to average price.
VERY_CHEAP The price is smaller or equal to 60 % compared to average price.
EXPENSIVE The price is greater or equal to 115 % and smaller than 140 % compared to average price.
VERY_EXPENSIVE The price is greater or equal to 140 % compared to average price.

The delta timer below the dividing line is the countdown timer until the minimum price. One rotation on Crown will switch price for temparature in Celcius (see 2nd picture), two for Weather Prognosis (THX to SMHI). Blue Temparature means below 0 degrees Celsius, green above. 

Plugin-Planning

Use the crown to step number of hours, this View will show you the optimal time to plugin in your device in order to minimize the average spotprice.

Power Usage & Cost

Shows power usage and cost by hour, day, week, month and year. Switch by using digital crown. ​ Drag gesture reveals the consumption/cost for a specific point in time. Blue title shows the aggregated sum of all bars shown in the chart. The yellow top part of the bar indicates the VAT part of the cost. Long tap toggles list view.

Power Production & Profit

Shows solar power production and profits by hour, day, week, month and year. Switch by using digital crown. ​ Drag gesture reveals the production/profit for a specific point in time. Green title shows the aggregated sum of all bars shown in the chart. The yellow top part of the bar indicates the VAT part of the profit. Long tap toggles list view.

Installation

  1. Signup for Tibber.
  2.  Create an Access Token for your account.
  3.  Install Wibber.
  4. Make sure a Nickname is associated with your home in Tibber. This is created in the Tibber iPhone app: My Account -> My Home -> Nickname.
  5. Enter Tibber Access token in Wibber

The easisest way to enter the Token, is to create a Text Replacement for it on the Iphone. This is done in Settings->General->Keyboard->Text Replacement.
