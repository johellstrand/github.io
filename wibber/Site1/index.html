WIBBER

Show the price for Electrical Power, your usage stats and costs and much more. Wibber is developed for Apple Watch and gets the data from Tibber. You have to be a Tibber customer and get the corresponding Access Token in order to use the App. 

Price Charts

Todays and tomorrows, last weeks, last month and last years price for electricity is shown. The digital crown changes view. Drag gesture reveals the price on a specific point in time. The shadowed area below the graph stops at the current time.

Clock View

Shows current price in the center. The coming 11 hours price is shown as analogue clock. The delta timer below the dividing line is the countdown timer until the minimum price. One rotation on Crown will switch price for temparature, two for Weather Prognosis (THX to SMHI). Blue Temparture menas below 0 degrees Celsius.

Plugin-Planning

Use the crown to step number of hours, this View will show you the optimal time to plugin in your device in order to minimize the average spotprice.

Historic Power Usage & Cost

Shows power usage and costs by hour, day, week, month.
